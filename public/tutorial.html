FuelTrak User Guide

Complete tutorial for Admin, Dispatcher, and Client users

Dashboard Overview

The dashboard gives you a real-time overview of all operations.

1 Stats Cards

View Pending, Approved, Loading, Completed counts at a glance.

2 Document Status

LTO, Fire Permit, DOST Calibration percentages. Click cards to view details.

Verify ATL (Approve/Reject)

  1. 1 Click Verify ATL on the dashboard Quick Actions.
  2. 2 Use the search bar to find specific ATLs by plate, company, or ATL code.
  3. 3 Review the ATL details: Company, SO#, Volume, Driver.
  4. 4 Click Approve (green) or Reject (red). Add optional remarks.
  5. 5 For cancellation requests, click Approve Cancel or Reject Cancel.

Loading Management

  1. 1 Click Loading on the dashboard.
  2. 2 Approved - Waiting: Click Start Loading to begin.
  3. 3 Ongoing Loading: Monitor active loads. Use Edit/Cancel/Done buttons.
  4. 4 Click Done to complete: Enter Actual Volume, Printed WC (12 digits), and TPS Series.
  5. 5 Confirm all details before final submission.

Truck Management

  1. 1 Click Trucks to view all trucks. Use search and status filter.
  2. 2 Click + Add Truck to register a new truck.
  3. 3 Fill in: Plate No, Make, Driver, Hauler, Capacity (L), TPS Count.
  4. 4 Set document expiry dates: LTO Registration, Fire Permit, DOST Calibration.
  5. 5 Click Save Truck. Edit or deactivate trucks anytime.

Reports & Analytics

  1. 1 Default view shows today's records. Use date filters to change range.
  2. 2 Filter by Status, Client, or Truck.
  3. 3 Click SI cell to toggle With SI / No SI.
  4. 4 Click Printed WC to edit the 12-digit withdrawal certificate.
  5. 5 Enter TPS From series. Use =formula in TPS To field (e.g., =001+11).
  6. 6 Click Export CSV to download all data. Click Print for hard copy.

Document Report

Click Doc Report to view all truck documents in one table with filters by document type and status (Valid/Expired/Missing). Printable and searchable.

Chat Feature

  1. 1 Click the blue chat bubble (bottom-right corner) on any page.
  2. 2 Select a client from the list to start chatting.
  3. 3 Messages refresh automatically every 5 seconds.

Client Dashboard

View your ATL summary: Total, Dispatched, Pending, Approved, and Failed/Cancelled counts.

Submit New ATL

  1. 1 Click New ATL button.
  2. 2 Enter Company, SO Number (optional), Scheduled Date, Volume (Liters).
  3. 3 Select With SI or No SI (Sales Invoice).
  4. 4 Enter Plate Number - it auto-verifies the truck and fills driver/hauler details.
  5. 5 You can override driver name and contact number for proxy drivers.
  6. 6 Review all details in the confirmation popup, then click OK to submit.
  7. 7 An ATL Code (e.g., AMC-000000001) is auto-generated.
Tip: You cannot submit a new ATL for a truck that already has a pending or approved ATL.

My ATLs (History)

  1. 1 View all your submitted ATLs with status badges.
  2. 2 Use search to find by plate, ATL code, or driver.
  3. 3 Filter by status (Pending, Approved, Dispatched, Completed, Cancelled).
  4. 4 Click View to see full ATL details.
  5. 5 Click Cancel on pending/approved ATLs to request cancellation.

Client Reports

  1. 1 View your ATL history with date and status filters.
  2. 2 Summary cards show total records, dispatched, failed, pending counts.
  3. 3 Click Print for hard copy or Export CSV to download.

Chat with Admin

  1. 1 Click the blue chat bubble (bottom-right corner).
  2. 2 Start chatting with admin/dispatcher directly.
